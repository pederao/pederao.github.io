Peder Olsen Mathematician and Space Farmer.

My passion is to use computer vision, machine learning and mathematics for agriculture and environmental monitoring. I worked on speech recognition at IBM Research and remote sensing and agriculture at IBM and Microsoft Research. I received my master's degree in mathematics from the Norwegian University of Science and Technology and my Phd in mathematics from the University of Michigan in Ann Arbor.
